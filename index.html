Behind the Lens

For fifteen years, I have chased the moment just before and just after the obvious one — the breath before a vow, the hush after a wave breaks. My work moves between continents and quiet rooms, always in search of the same thing: light that tells the truth.

Based between Lisbon and the coastline, I take on a limited number of commissions each year so every frame gets the attention it deserves.
